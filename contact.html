Our Partners

Tespa

Tespa is a network of college clubs founded to promote gaming culture and host the best college esports events and competitions. Together, our members are growing collegiate esports across North America both on campus and inside packed arenas.

Twitch

Founded in June 2011, Twitch is the world’s leading social video platform and community for gamers. Each month, more than 100 million community members gather to watch and talk about video games with more than 1.7 million broadcasters. Twitch’s live and video on demand platform caters to the entire video game industry, including game developers, publishers, media outlets, events, casual content creators, and the entire esports scene.

Vasculogic LLC.

Vasculogic is dedicated to developing and commercializing image-guided medical devices and diagnostic products. Our mission is to revolutionize vascular access by developing products that dramatically improve patient care and practitioner safety.

Kite and Key

The history of Rutgers is the history of the nation, a story that begins in the political maelstrom of colonial America; hurtles through the Civil War, the Industrial Revolution, and two world wars; wrestles with the social upheaval of the second half of the 20th century; and emerges in the fast-paced universe of today’s global digital age. Delve into the rich history of Rutgers and experience nearly 250 years of tradition and innovation. It is with that sense of tradition and innovation that we take the motivation for kite+key, the Rutgers Tech Store. Benjamin Franklin conducted the famous lightning experiment. As an adult, his son, William Franklin signed the charter that ultimately created Rutgers. kite+key, the Rutgers Tech Store, is named for their tools of invention + discovery.
